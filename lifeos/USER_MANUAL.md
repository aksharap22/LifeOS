# User Manual

# LifeOS - Personal Optimization Engine

## Introduction

LifeOS helps users discover which habits and routines work best for them through structured personal experiments.

Instead of relying on generic advice, users collect data about their own lifestyle and receive personalized recommendations.

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Register

Create an account using:

* Email Address
* Username
* Password

---

### Step 2: Create an Experiment

Click on "Create Experiment" and enter:

* Experiment Name
* Hypothesis
* Duration
* Metrics to Track

Example:

Experiment:
Morning Study vs Night Study

Hypothesis:
I learn better in the morning.

Duration:
14 Days

Metrics:

* Focus
* Productivity
* Retention

---

## Daily Logging

Every day, record:

* Mood Score
* Energy Level
* Productivity Score
* Sleep Hours
* Exercise Duration
* Notes

The system stores and visualizes your progress automatically.

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard displays:

* Active Experiments
* Completed Experiments
* Daily Trends
* Productivity Analytics
* Progress Charts

---

## Results Analysis

After completing an experiment, LifeOS generates:

* Experiment Summary
* Statistical Analysis
* Performance Comparison
* Hypothesis Validation

Example:

Result:
Morning Study improved retention by 18%.

Conclusion:
Hypothesis Supported.

---

## Personalized Insights

Examples:

* You are most productive between 8 AM and 11 AM.
* Sleeping less than 7 hours reduces productivity.
* Exercise improves mood scores.

---

## Personal Operating Manual

Based on completed experiments, LifeOS creates a customized profile that includes:

* Best Sleep Schedule
* Best Study Hours
* Best Exercise Time
* Recommended Screen Time
* Productivity Optimization Tips
